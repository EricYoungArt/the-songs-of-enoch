In the beginning, when the cosmos sang the Song of Death,
Enoch walked the path between light and shadow.

The Songs of Enoch is a mythic graphic narrative born from a deep inner calling to reimagine the origin of life, humanity, and the divine. Through evocative visuals and poetic storytelling, it retells the Genesis creation story through a shamanic lens—unfolding through the eyes of Enoch, the firstborn son of Cain, witness to the fall of man and the mysteries that shaped the cosmos.

This series invites readers into a world where myth becomes mirror—where ancient questions still echo and forgotten truths rise again. It speaks to those who seek beauty in story, depth in symbol, and meaning in the unknown.
